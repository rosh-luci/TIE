ABOUT US

Infosys Limited is a global leader in consulting, technology, outsourcing, and next-generation services. Established in 1981 by seven engineers in Pune, India, the company is headquartered in Bangalore. Over the years, Infosys has grown to become one of the largest IT services firms globally, serving clients across more than 50 countries.

Infosys provides a wide range of services including software development, maintenance, and independent validation services to companies in the finance, insurance, manufacturing, and other sectors. The company is renowned for its innovation and quality, leveraging cutting-edge technologies like artificial intelligence, machine learning, cloud computing, and digital transformation to solve complex business challenges.

A hallmark of Infosys is its commitment to sustainability and corporate responsibility. The company actively pursues initiatives to reduce its carbon footprint and improve the well-being of its employees and communities. Financially, Infosys is a strong performer, consistently delivering robust revenue and profit growth. It is publicly traded on both the New York Stock Exchange and the Bombay Stock Exchange. With a diverse and inclusive workforce, Infosys continues to drive change and shape the future of technology and business.
